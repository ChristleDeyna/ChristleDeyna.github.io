Famous Delicacies

Bicol Express

A spicy dish made with pork, shrimp, and coconut milk. A must-try delicacy!

Laing

Taro leaves cooked in coconut milk with chili and shrimp paste. A flavorful dish from the region!

Sili Ice Cream

A unique ice cream flavor made from the famous Bicol chili. Sweet and spicy at the same time!

Stunning Islands

Catanduanes

Known as the "Land of the Howling Winds", Catanduanes offers picturesque beaches and lush mountains.

Malilipot Island

An untouched paradise perfect for nature lovers and beach goers.

Masbate

Famous for its rodeo festival, Masbate is a tropical haven with beautiful beaches and heritage sites.

Famous Places

Mayon Volcano

Known for its near-perfect cone shape, Mayon is a must-see natural wonder in Albay.

Donsol

A tranquil town famous for whale shark encounters and clear waters, perfect for eco-tourism.

Cagsawa Ruins

A historical landmark with a stunning view of Mayon Volcano and remnants of a church destroyed by an eruption.
